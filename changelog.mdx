## Recent Updates

<Update label="2024-11-05" description="v1.3.0" tags={["feature", "improvement"]}>

## New Features

- Added AI-powered SDE (Seller's Discretionary Earnings) analysis directly from uploaded financials.
- Integrated DocuSign eSignatures for client approvals on CIMs.
- Custom branding now supports your logo and color scheme (`#ed8769` by default).

## Improvements

- CIM generation speed increased by `>40%` using optimized website scraping.
- Enhanced password protection with expiration dates and view limits.

## Bug Fixes

- Fixed CRM contact sync issues with Outlook and Google Workspace.
- Resolved custom URL sharing errors on mobile devices.

</Update>

## How to Stay Updated

Follow these steps to ensure you never miss a release.
